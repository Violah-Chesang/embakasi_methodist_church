Church Membership

Gaining membership in to Embakasi Methodist Church is by sacrament of baptism and service of reception after proper preparation by our Ministers and Catechism team. It is for any person who sincerely desires to be saved from sins through faith in the lord Jesus Christ and evidence the same in life and conduct and who seek to have fellowship with Christ Himself and His people.
