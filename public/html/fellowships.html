Our fellowships

A sheep is safer among 'Fellow-Sheep' than alone(Fellowship)

Men Fellowship

Embakasi Methodist Men Fellowship is made up of all the men. They are involved with mentoring the young generation and building each other spiritually.

Women Fellowship

Embakasi Methodist Women Fellowship is a vibrant group of women of all ages in the church. They meet every third sunday of the month to fellowship in the afternoon.

Youth Fellowship

This is a special group comprising of the youth. They have their own services in their youth church.

Junior Church

The Junior Sunday School (JSS) comprise of kids between the age bracket of 2 and 12 years.


At Embakasi Methodist Church, the children get an opportunity to learn about the word of God using Bible-based teachings. They have qualified teachers for every group as they are grouped according to their ages.


The children are encouraged to have a personal relationship with God. When they get to 12 years, they go through catechism and after that they are received as full members of the church.
